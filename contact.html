Upload a LEGO Model

Want to upload your creation? Follow these steps.

  • 1. Build your model
  • 2. Take a picture of your model
  • 3. Write a short description of your model. Include the cretion's name, your name, number of bricks, and time it took to create
  • 4. Send your picture and description to this email allanpleaseaddemail@pmail.com

In around a week your model should be uploaded on BrickBook

Upload a LEGO Instruction Manual

Want to upload some LEGO instruction manuals? Follow these steps

  • 1. Create a LEGO instruction manual. You can draw it on paper but I recommend using an online program called LEGO Digital Designer and take screenshots of your model and put them together in a doccument.
  • 2. Write a short description of the model the manual is showing to build. Include the creation's name, your name, number of bricks, and number of steps.
  • 3. Send your manual and description to this email allanpleaseaddemail@pmail.com

In around a week your manual should be uploaded on Brickbook.
